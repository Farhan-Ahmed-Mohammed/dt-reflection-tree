## Design Rationale

This reflection tree is designed across three psychological axes: Locus of Control, Contribution vs Entitlement, and Radius of Concern.

### Axis 1 (Locus)
Questions identify whether the user attributes outcomes to internal effort or external factors. Branching allows different reflections for agency vs lack of control.

### Axis 2 (Contribution)
Focus shifts from self to contribution. Questions identify whether the user contributed, stayed neutral, or expected more.

### Axis 3 (Radius)
The final axis expands perspective from self → team → customers, encouraging broader thinking.

### Design Choices
- Used fixed options to ensure determinism
- Decision nodes map clearly to reflections
- Bridges ensure smooth conversational flow
- Reflections avoid judgment and encourage awareness
